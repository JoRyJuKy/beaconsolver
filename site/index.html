Beacon Solver

Instructions:

  • Use colored buttons to spawn stars
  • Click and drag to move stars
  • Right-click to remove stars
  • Right-click and drag between two stars to create/remove a connection between them
  • Double-click to set the primary star
  • The position of the stars doesn't matter, just the connections
