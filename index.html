Beacon Solver

Instructions:

  • Use colored buttons to spawn stars
  • Click and drag to move stars
  • Right-click to remove stars
  • Right-click and drag between two stars to create a connection/warp lane between them
  • To activate the solver, drag a connection between the primary star (one circled in the in-game beacon image) and the "Solve" button at the bottom of the area
